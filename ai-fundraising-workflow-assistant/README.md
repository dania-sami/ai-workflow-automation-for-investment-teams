# AI Fundraising & Investment Workflow Assistant

I built this project to explore how AI can be applied to real workflows in investment and fundraising environments, rather than staying limited to isolated demos.

The goal was to create something practical: a workflow assistant that helps with research briefs, meeting preparation, CRM notes, follow-up drafts, reporting, and workflow mapping. I wanted to focus on the parts of day-to-day work where consistency, clarity, and speed matter most.

## What the project does

The app includes six workflow modules:

- **Workflow Mapper** to identify bottlenecks and automation opportunities
- **Research Brief Generator** for structured summaries and investment-style notes
- **Meeting Prep** for agendas, talking points, risk areas, and questions
- **CRM & Follow-up** for turning meeting notes into clean updates and email drafts
- **Reporting Studio** for executive summaries and slide-ready outputs
- **Sales Support** for quick CSV-based analysis and presentation prep

## Why I built it

What interested me most was the idea of using AI not only to generate text, but to improve how professionals work. In environments where people deal with research, relationships, reporting, and time pressure, useful AI should make workflows clearer and more repeatable.

This project was my way of exploring that in a focused, hands-on way.

## Tech stack

- Python
- Streamlit
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

## Notes

The current version is designed as a practical workflow prototype. It focuses on structured outputs, prompt-oriented logic, and usability. I intentionally kept it simple and readable so it can be tested, improved, and adapted to different operational contexts.
